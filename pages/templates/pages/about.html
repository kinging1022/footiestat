WHO WE ARE

About FootieStat

FootieStat is a football statistics and analysis platform built by fans, for fans. We cover post-match breakdowns, tactical analysis, and editorial coverage from every major league on the planet.

OUR MISSION

Football deserves better data. FootieStat was founded on a simple idea: every fan should have access to the same depth of tactical insight and match intelligence that professional analysts use — presented clearly, without the clutter.

We dig into completed matches — pulling apart the numbers, the patterns, and the decisions that determined the result. Whether you follow the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, or dozens of other competitions worldwide, FootieStat has you covered.

OUR VALUES

ACCURACY

Every statistic is sourced from verified data providers. We do not publish information we cannot stand behind.

DEPTH

We go beyond the scoreline — breaking down tactics, individual performances, and the patterns that shaped each match.

COMMUNITY

We write for fans. Our editorial voice is honest, knowledgeable, and free from commercial bias in our analysis.

TRANSPARENCY

We are upfront about how we fund our platform, how we handle your data, and who writes our content.

HOW WE ARE FUNDED

FootieStat is currently a free platform. To keep it that way long term, we intend to introduce advertising in the future. When we do, we will only work with ad partners whose formats respect our users — no pop-ups, no auto-playing audio, no misleading creatives, and nothing designed to trick you into clicking.

Our editorial content will always remain fully independent from any commercial relationships. Advertisers will have no influence over our match analysis, tactical breakdowns, or any other content on the site.

CONTACT US

We welcome feedback, corrections, partnership enquiries, and general questions from our readers. Our team aims to respond to all messages within 48 hours on business days.

For advertising and partnership opportunities, please mention this in your subject line and we will route your message to the appropriate team.
