This Privacy Policy explains how FootieStat ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit footiestat.com. By using our website you agree to the practices described in this policy. Please read it carefully.

1. WHO WE ARE

FootieStat is a football statistics and news platform. We are the data controller responsible for personal information collected through this website. If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact us using the details in Section 11.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you actively provide to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you submit our contact form
  • Any additional information you voluntarily include in messages or enquiries

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser type, version, and language settings
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited, time on page, and navigation path
  • Referring website or search query that brought you to our site
  • Date and time of your visit

Information from Cookies

We and our advertising and analytics partners collect information through cookies and similar tracking technologies. See Section 4 for full details.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Legal Basis Details
Delivering website content Legitimate interest Providing you with match statistics, analysis, and editorial content
Responding to enquiries Contract / consent Using your name and email to reply to contact form submissions
Displaying advertisements Legitimate interest / consent Showing relevant adverts through trusted advertising networks to fund our free service (when advertising is active)
Analytics and improvement Legitimate interest Understanding how the site is used so we can improve it
Security and fraud prevention Legitimate interest Detecting and preventing abuse, spam, or malicious activity
Legal compliance Legal obligation Meeting our obligations under applicable laws

4. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use cookies and similar technologies. For full details on all cookies used on this website — including how to opt out — please see our Cookie Policy.

In summary, the types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — used to measure site traffic and user behaviour (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — may be used by third-party advertising networks to serve relevant advertisements (when advertising is active)

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using the opt-out tools referenced in our Cookie Policy.

5. ADVERTISING & THIRD-PARTY NETWORKS

FootieStat intends to display advertising in the future to sustain the platform as a free service. When advertising is introduced, we will work with third-party advertising networks that may use cookies, device identifiers, and browsing data to serve relevant advertisements.

Each advertising partner we work with will be an independent data controller for the data they collect. We will update this section with the specific networks in use and links to their privacy policies when advertising becomes active.

When advertising is active, you will be able to opt out of personalised advertising through the following industry opt-out tools:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

Opting out of personalised ads will not remove advertisements from the site — you will see non-personalised (contextual) ads instead.

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain different categories of data for different periods:

  • Contact form submissions — retained for up to 12 months, then securely deleted
  • Server logs (including IP addresses) — retained for up to 90 days for security purposes
  • Analytics data — retained for up to 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Advertising data — governed by the respective advertising network's own retention policies (when advertising is active)

When data is no longer required for its stated purpose and there is no legal basis for retaining it, we will securely delete or anonymise it.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — request we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

FootieStat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
